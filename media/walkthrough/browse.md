# Browse your monorepo

Open the Tachikoma activity bar on the left to explore your monorepo
hierarchy: galaxies, systems, and spaces. Pick a context and use
"Open in Workspace" to mirror it locally.

- [Open the Contexts view](command:tachikomaContextTree.focus)
- Right click a context to create files, folders, or open a remote terminal.
- Cache syncs in the background as you navigate.
